Dream

Every project starts with a dream, an initial idea or concept that ignites and excites. At f8 Design & Build we embrace the dreamer in all of our clients, it brings out the best in our work and allows us to create a final product that you enjoy and we are proud of.

Consult

Once you've solidified your dream, allow us to consult with you to turn your dreams into a reality. At f8 Design & Build we sit with you to make sure you understand the best route towards completing your project. We make sure we stick within your budget while still offering quality service using quality materials..

Design

Allow us to do all of the design work in this pre-build phase of your project. At f8 Design & Build we use the best architects to draft accurate and timely design plans. A great design is an integral part of making sure that the job is done right the first time. During this entire process, we walk you through each phase as a good design is the cornerstone of lasting memories.

Build

The build phase is the final part of your project and the most important. Rest assured that your project is in great hands as we craft your dreams into reality. We work tirelessly to make sure that timelines are respected while you experience the utmost in courtesy and customer service at all times during the build.
